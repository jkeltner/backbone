## How to Use This Document

This is the recording document—structured enough to keep you on track, loose enough to allow genuine conversation. It's built from the raw research and your episode notes.

**What's here:**
- Scripted intros/transitions for each section
- Key beats and talking points in order
- Specific stats, quotes, and details to hit
- Cues for who's driving each section
- Suggested handoffs between hosts

**What's NOT here:**
- Word-for-word scripts for everything (unless you want them)
- Every detail from the research (just what you're using)
