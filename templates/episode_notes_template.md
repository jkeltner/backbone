## How to Use This Document

This is your working document for shaping the episode. Use the raw research as your source material, but this is where you synthesize, prioritize, and make editorial decisions.

**Workflow:**
1. Review the raw research file
2. Pull in the best material here, add your own notes and connections
3. Trade ideas, reorganize, debate what to include
4. This becomes the brief for the script/outline

**What goes here:**
- What you definitely want to include
- Connections and throughlines you've identified
- Questions for each other
- Notes on tone, pacing, emphasis
- Ideas for how to present something

**What stays in raw research:**
- Exhaustive detail you might not use
- Source citations and verification notes
- The full data tables and timelines
